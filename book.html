The Run: A Barefoot Journey For Change.

In 2021, Sav designed a 500KM barefoot run to raise funds and awareness for a leadership education program in Uganda, Africa. What began as an idea in the middle of the night exploded into a movement through the community and a village assembling to raise $12,000 for the cause. Sav reflects on the brutality of this undertaking; including the preparations, daily experiences and the battle through hell to accomplish a task many deemed impossible.

Run4Rescue

On December 2ND 2023, Sav will begin a 25HR run to fundraise for the SA Foundation - a local organization committed to survivors of human trafficking. The run will take place in the Okanagan and be open for spectators. 

As with all ultras, they take an entire village to put on and we're excited to invite you to be apart of this amazing journey. This will be the first event hosted by Sav that will be open to the public. Space is limited so please inform Sav quickly if you'd like to participate.
